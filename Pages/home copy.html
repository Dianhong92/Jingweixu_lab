Multiscale imaging across the tree of life!

We aim to explore how cells efficiently reshape their metabolic network when stressed in adverse conditions, by intergrating a multiscale imageing pipeline (e.g. cryoEM, in situ cryoET, expansion microscopy and light microscopy) with multi-omics analysis.
